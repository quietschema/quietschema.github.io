Commercial information

Clear terms for digital services.

Pricing and payment

Pricing depends on project scope, complexity, file volume, and delivery requirements.

  • A quotation is provided before the project starts.
  • Payment may be requested through a PayPal invoice.
  • Any applicable transaction fees are handled through PayPal.

Delivery

Services are delivered digitally. Typical delivery time is between 2 and 7 business days, depending on the project.

  • Delivery timing is confirmed in advance.
  • Large or complex projects may require a separate schedule.
  • No physical shipping is involved.

Cancellation and refunds

A full refund may be provided when a project is cancelled before work begins.

  • After work begins, refunds are reviewed based on completed work.
  • Delivered custom digital work is generally non-refundable.
  • Reasonable corrections are available when the delivered work does not match the agreed scope.

Data handling

Client files are used only for the agreed service and are not intentionally shared with unrelated third parties.

  • Clients should remove unnecessary personal or sensitive data.
  • Retention and deletion requirements can be agreed before work begins.
  • Confidentiality expectations should be stated in the project requirements.
